How do I buy tickets?

Buy online through the Tickets page for the best value and fast entry. You can also purchase at the gate, but online pre-booking helps avoid queues.

Are parking and food available?

Yes. Parking is free with the Family Package, and the park has several cafés, snack stalls, and sit-down restaurants with vegetarian and kid-friendly options.

What safety measures are in place?

Rides are inspected daily, staff members follow park safety protocols, and hygiene stations are available throughout the park.
