Welcome to Wonderland

Wonderland Theme Park is your ultimate destination for thrill and fun. Welcome to Wonderla Amusement Park, where fun knows no bounds and thrills await around every corner. Established with the vision of providing unparalleled entertainment experiences, Wonderla is renowned for its world-class rides, attractions, and impeccable service.

With locations in Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Kochi, Wonderla has become synonymous with excitement and adventure.

Whether you're seeking adrenaline-pumping thrills or family-friendly fun, Wonderla Bengaluru has something for everyone. From gravity-defying roller coasters to refreshing water slides, Wonderla rides and attractions promise an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Known as the high-tech hub of India, Bangalore boasts a fast-paced life. But there's a world of fun waiting for you, where you can take a step back, relax and enjoy the present that life is.

Strap in, for you are in for a wild ride brimming with joy and crazy happiness, with the 60+ rides at Wonderla Bengaluru!

Fun from the get-go is what we like and that's exactly what we deliver. The moment you step in, you will feel like you're in a whole other dimension. It's almost like paradise—full of laughter, merrymaking, and thrills!
